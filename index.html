PlayBlocker

Stage blocking software for theatre directors.

Upload a floor plan of your set and an HTML-formatted script, then place your actors on the diagram and annotate the script with movement markers as you work through a blocking session.

Every position and movement is saved to the server so sessions can be resumed exactly where you left off.
